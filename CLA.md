## How to Sign

This CLA is enforced automatically via the [CLA Assistant](https://github.com/contributor-assistant/github-action) bot on the MIXI GitHub repository.

When you open a Pull Request:

1. The CLA bot will post a comment on your PR.
2. You will be asked to confirm your agreement by commenting `I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA`.
3. Your signature is recorded and linked to your GitHub identity.
4. The PR check turns green and your contribution can be reviewed and merged.

You only need to sign once. All future contributions are covered by the same agreement.
